Privacy Policy

Deck Diver — Fox & Fairy Games LLC

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

Introduction

Fox & Fairy Games LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates Deck Diver ("the Game"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

We respect your privacy and are committed to collecting only what is necessary to improve the Game.

Information We Collect

Gameplay Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous gameplay data. This includes:

  • Game events such as which characters are selected, levels reached, rooms completed, items used, and boss encounters
  • Session duration and play frequency
  • General device information such as device type, operating system, and screen size
  • App performance data such as crash reports and load times

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal contact information
  • Precise geographic location or GPS data
  • Photos, contacts, messages, or any data from your device
  • Payment or financial information
  • Login credentials or account passwords

The Game does not require you to create an account or sign in.

How We Use This Information

We use anonymous gameplay analytics solely to:

  • Understand how players interact with the Game
  • Identify bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Balance game mechanics and improve the player experience
  • Track aggregate usage trends such as average and peak play times

We do not sell, share, or rent any data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses Google Firebase Analytics, which is operated by Google LLC. Firebase may collect certain technical data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. We have configured our Firebase settings to minimize data collection.

Data Retention

Gameplay analytics data is retained in aggregate form. No individual user profiles are created or maintained.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because we do not collect personal information from any users, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

Local Storage

The Game stores your gameplay progress (such as character unlocks, settings, and saved runs) locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
